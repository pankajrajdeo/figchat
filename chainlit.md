# Welcome to LungChat by LungMAP! 🌟🧬

Hi there, Researcher! 👋 We're excited to have you join us. LungChat by LungMAP is a dedicated assistant designed to help you interactively explore and visualize **single-cell RNA-seq (scRNA-seq) datasets** from LungMAP with an extensive range of plotting capabilities.

## What is LungChat by LungMAP? 🤔

LungChat by LungMAP is your interactive platform for:
- **Exploring LungMAP Datasets:** Delve into the metadata and structure of our datasets.
- **Generating Visualizations:** Produce a variety of plots—including UMAP plots, heatmaps, violin plots, dot plots, cell frequency boxplots, gene interaction networks, Venn diagrams, and UpSet plots—to reveal hidden patterns and relationships.
- **Comparing Studies:** Contrast key differences between studies such as the **Fetal BPD Study** and the **Infant BPD Study** using advanced comparative tools.
- **Advanced Analysis:** Leverage comparative analysis and enhanced metadata insights for deeper exploration and to uncover subtle nuances within the data.

---

## Key Datasets 📂

1. **Human Lung Cell Atlas (HLCA) Metacells**
   - A comprehensive atlas of 50K metacells.
   - Source: Human Lung Cell Atlas Initiative.

2. **Fetal Lung Development (HCA)**
   - A multiomic atlas covering 5–22 post-conception weeks.
   - Source: Wellcome HCA Strategic Science Support.

3. **Infant BPD Study (Sun Lab)**
   - Investigates bronchopulmonary dysplasia (BPD) in infants.
   - Source: LungMAP (Sun Lab).

4. **Fetal BPD Study (Sucre Lab)**
   - Explores molecular dynamics driving BPD and pulmonary hypertension in preterm infants.
   - Source: LungMAP (Sucre Lab).

## Current Capabilities 🚀

LungChat by LungMAP currently supports:
- UMAP Plots
- Heatmaps
- Violin Plots
- Dot Plots
- Cell Frequency Boxplots
- Gene Interaction Networks
- Venn Diagrams and UpSet Plots

These robust visualization tools enable you to examine datasets from multiple perspectives and gain comprehensive insights through a streamlined suite of functions.
